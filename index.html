My Role Model

Prince Ea (Richard Williams):




About the videos:


Richard William goes by the speaker name of Prince Ea. He is an American-spoken poet, Youtuber, motivational speaker and my role model. The two videos listed here are from his youtube channel; they both tackle important topics present in our society currently. The first video focuses on labels in society, it is essential to understand labels as they affect many people’s views. He promotes the saying, "Labels were Made Up to Divide us." The purpose of this video is to drop labels in society. This is an educational and inspiring work that enhances my thinking.

The second video touches upon the use of technology; media is all around us, and technology is more prevalent than ever. Prince Ea talks about "auto-correcting humanity.” These two videos are what really got me inspired in speaking. The thought of words being able to inspire so many astounds me
